A Victor, Not a Victim

Hardship does not define your finish line. Being a Victor means keeping your eyes forward and moving toward the mission, no matter what lies behind you.

The "X" Promise

Founded in honor of Lucianne Horace, whose sacrifice proved that resilience is an action. Her legacy is the heartbeat of this organization.

Community Leadership

We believe in active service. From mentorship in youth shelters to supporting disciplined growth in JROTC, we are building a foundation for success.

Our Impact

Victor Global Initiative is committed to empowering at-risk youth by providing mentorship, stability, and opportunity.

  • Supporting youth shelters and transitional programs
  • Developing mentorship programs for young leaders
  • Supporting school JROTC leadership programs
  • Promoting resilience and responsibility in the next generation
