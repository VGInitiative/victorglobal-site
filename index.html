The Heartbeat of VGI

A Legacy Rooted in Resilience

In 1992, while living in a shelter in Elizabeth, New Jersey, Pierre-Richard Victor learned that resilience is an action, not just a word. Today, he carries his mother's legacy forward—transforming his journey from survival into a mission of service for every child who feels left behind.

Our Mission

To equip the next generation with the resilience to look past their struggles and the resources to build their future.

Our Philosophy

A Victor is not someone who never faces hardship, but someone who refuses to let hardship define their finish line.
