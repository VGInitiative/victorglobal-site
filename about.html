Our Leadership

Pierre-Richard Victor

Founder & President

Born in Port-au-Prince, Haiti and raised in the systems of New York and New Jersey, Pierre-Richard knows the power of resilience firsthand.

Arriving in the United States at age 15, he navigated homelessness and shelters while carrying the memory of his mother’s sacrifice.

Today, he is a U.S. Army veteran and a dedicated United Airlines Flight Attendant with over 25 years of service. His journey is the blueprint for the "Victor, Not a Victim" philosophy.

Service Record & Medals

  • 🎖️ Joint Service Commendation Medal
  • 🎖️ Army Commendation Medal
  • 🎖️ Global War on Terrorism Expeditionary Medal
  • 🎖️ Saint Christopher Medal
