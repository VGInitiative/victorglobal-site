Pierre-Richard Victor

Founder & President

Born in Port-au-Prince, Haiti and raised in the systems of New York and New Jersey, Pierre-Richard knows the power of resilience firsthand.

Arriving in the United States at age 15, he navigated homelessness and shelters while carrying the memory of his mother’s sacrifice.

Today he is a U.S. Army veteran and United Airlines Flight Attendant of over 25 years.

Service Record

  • Army Commendation Medal
  • Global War on Terrorism Expeditionary Medal
