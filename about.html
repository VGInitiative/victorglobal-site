Future Horizons

Strategic Roadmap 2026-2031

VGI is moving from foundational setup to national and global expansion. Our vision is a world where every child is defined by their potential, not their past.

Phase 1: Year 1

Foundation

Focusing on our home base in Florida. Establishing resource allocation for local youth shelters and school JROTC programs to build immediate capability.

Phase 2: Year 3

National Expansion

Scaling the Victor Mentorship Model across the United States. Finalizing toolkits for nationwide deployment to ensure hardship is a chapter, not the conclusion.

Phase 3: Year 5

Global Reach

Extending our mission to communities in the Caribbean and Africa. Building international bridges over obstacles for children globally.

Status: Mission Planning & Resource Allocation

Last Update: March 2026

We acknowledge the past, but we do not dwell in it. Our focus is locked on the horizon and the potential of what comes next. By creating capability rather than dependency, we ensure every resource provided is a tool for a young person's victory story.
