Pierre-Richard Victor

Founder & President

Born in Haiti and raised in the systems of NY/NJ, Pierre-Richard knows resilience firsthand. Arriving at 15, he navigated shelters while honoring his mother’s sacrifice.

Today he is a U.S. Army veteran and United Airlines Flight Attendant of 25+ years, bringing a unique perspective on global service and discipline to VGI.
