From the Streets to the Stage: VGI Officially Launches

MANGO, FL — Today marks a pivotal milestone for the community as the Victor Global Initiative (VGI) officially opens its doors. Founded by Pierre-Richard Victor, the organization is built on a mission: transforming at-risk youth into "Victors."

“I know what it’s like to arrive in this country with nothing but a dream,” says Victor. “VGI is about providing a global perspective to children who have only ever seen the four walls of their struggle.”
