Our Initiatives

Supporting Vulnerable Youth

Having served as a foster parent for over a decade, our founder understands the critical need for stability. VGI is dedicated to establishing partnerships with and funding local youth shelters to provide not just a roof, but a pathway to independence for at-risk children.

Fostering Discipline & Duty

We actively develop plans to support school JROTC programs. Structured environments, combined with mentorship initiatives, give young people the tools to become responsible, community-minded leaders.

The "Victor" Mentorship Model

Led by Pierre-Richard and Jeremiah, our mentorship programs teach young men and women that their past does not dictate their future. Programs focus on emotional resilience, financial literacy, and career planning to prepare the next generation for success.
