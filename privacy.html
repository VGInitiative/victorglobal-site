Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Victor Global Initiative (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information from visitors and donors on our website (www.victorglobal.org).

By using our website, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information when you:

  • Make a donation
  • Sign up for newsletters or volunteer opportunities
  • Contact us via forms or email

Types of information collected:

  • Name, email address, mailing address, phone number
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe or PayPal Giving Fund)
  • IP address and browser type for website analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Processing donations and sending receipts
  • Sending updates, newsletters, or event invitations (if you opt-in)
  • Responding to inquiries or volunteer applications
  • Improving our website and services

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies or analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to:

  • Understand website traffic and user behavior
  • Improve website performance and content

You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party service providers for:

  • Payment processing (Stripe, PayPal Giving Fund)
  • Email communications (Mailchimp, Google Workspace)
  • Analytics (Google Analytics)

These providers are authorized to use your personal information only to perform services on our behalf.

6. Data Security

  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information.
  • Personal data is encrypted during online transmission using SSL.
  • While we strive to protect your information, no website is completely secure.

7. Sharing Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with:

  • Legal authorities if required by law
  • Service providers to process donations or support website functionality

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request corrections to your personal information
  • Opt-out of receiving marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at: victorp@victorglobal.org

9. Children’s Privacy

Our website and programs are intended for individuals 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be posted on this page with the date updated.
