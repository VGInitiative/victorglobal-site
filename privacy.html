Integrity & Transparency

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

At Victor Global Initiative (VGI), integrity is the foundation of everything we do. We are committed to protecting the data of our donors, volunteers, and partners with the same discipline we apply to our mentorship programs.

1. Information Stewardship

We collect personal information only when it is essential to fulfilling our mission. This includes data provided when you:

  • Invest in our mission via our secure donation portal.
  • Engage with us for volunteer or mentorship opportunities.
  • Request updates via our secure partnership forms.

VGI will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We treat your trust as a sacred oath.

2. Secure Data Processing

We utilize industry-leading partners to ensure your data is handled with "Aviation-Grade" security:

  • Financials: All donations are processed via Stripe. VGI does not store sensitive credit card data on our internal servers.
  • Communication: Inquiries are managed through secure Microsoft infrastructure to ensure confidentiality.
  • Encryption: Personal data is encrypted during transmission using SSL (Secure Sockets Layer).

3. Your Rights & Control

Transparency is a core value of the Victor Standard. You have the right to:

  • Review the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections or the deletion of your information.
  • Opt-out of any mission updates or newsletters at any time.

4. Children’s Privacy

Given our mission to support foster youth and at-risk children, we take extra care regarding privacy. Our public website is intended for individuals 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 through this platform.

5. Contact & Governance

If you have questions regarding this policy or how your data is used to build capability for youth, please reach out to our leadership team.
