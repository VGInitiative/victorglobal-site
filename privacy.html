Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

1. Introduction

Victor Global Initiative (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information from visitors and donors on our website (www.victorglobal.org).

By using our website, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information when you perform the following actions:

  • Make a donation
  • Sign up for newsletters or volunteer opportunities
  • Contact us via forms or email

Types of information collected: Name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and secure payment information (processed by Stripe or PayPal Giving Fund).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Processing donations and sending tax-compliant receipts.
  • Sending updates or newsletters (only if you opt-in).
  • Improving our services and responding to inquiries.

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies to understand website traffic and improve performance. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may be affected.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted partners for payment processing (Stripe), email (Mailchimp), and analytics. These providers are authorized to use your information only to perform services on our behalf.

6. Data Security

Personal data is encrypted during transmission using SSL. While we implement reasonable security measures, no website is completely secure.

7. Sharing Information

We may share information with legal authorities if required by law or with service providers necessary to process donations.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, review, or request corrections to your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at: info@victorglobal.org

9. Children’s Privacy

Our website is intended for individuals 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Updates

We may update this policy periodically. The current version will always be posted here with the update date.
