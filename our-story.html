Why We Exist

Victor Global Initiative (VGI) was born on the streets and in the quiet corners of youth shelters. Our founder, Pierre-Richard Victor, lived the reality that VGI seeks to transform. Having experienced childhood homelessness, he developed deep empathy for children facing similar obstacles.

After serving in the U.S. Army and navigating the world as a flight attendant for 25+ years, Pierre-Richard committed ten years to being a foster parent. VGI is the culmination of that journey, a beacon of resilience for every child who has ever felt overlooked.

Lucianne Horace Legacy

The heartbeat of VGI is the legacy of Lucianne Horace. Her spirit of perseverance and unwavering belief in the potential of the next generation are the foundations upon which we stand.

Through her influence, we learned that a "Victor" is not defined by where they start, but by their willingness to keep moving forward. We carry her name into every shelter we support and every mentorship session we lead.

"We are building Victors, not victims. That is the Promise."

Tampa Bay to the World

Based in Tampa Area, Florida, our mission begins at home. Over the next five to ten years, we are dedicated to creating a meaningful impact across the United States. Our ultimate vision is global—extending our resources to communities in the Caribbean and Africa to ensure that hardship is a chapter in their story, not the conclusion.
