"Empowering dreams to flourish under the Florida sun."

Why We Exist

The Victor Global Initiative (VGI) was not born in a boardroom; it was born on the streets and in the quiet corners of youth shelters. Our founder, Pierre-Richard Victor, lived the very reality that VGI now seeks to transform. Having experienced the profound challenges of childhood homelessness and the uncertainty of life in shelters, Pierre-Richard developed a deep empathy for children facing similar obstacles.

This lived experience became the fuel for a life of service. After serving in the U.S. Army and navigating the world as a global flight attendant, Pierre-Richard committed nearly ten years of his life to being a foster parent—eventually adopting his two sons as a single father. VGI is the culmination of that journey: a beacon of resilience for every child who has ever felt overlooked.

The Legacy of Lucianne Horace

Every mission has a heartbeat, and for VGI, that heartbeat is the legacy of Lucianne Horace. Her spirit of perseverance and her unwavering belief in the potential of the next generation are the foundations upon which this organization stands.

It is through her influence that we learned that a "Victor" is not defined by where they start, but by their willingness to keep moving forward. We carry her name and her legacy into every shelter we support and every mentorship session we lead.

The Victor Promise

"The Victor Promise" is our sacred oath to the youth we serve. It is a commitment to three core pillars:

  • Presence: We show up for foster children when the system cannot.
  • Preparation: We provide military-grade discipline and professional standards to prepare them for global stages.
  • Perspective: We help them see beyond their current "four walls" to the limitless opportunities of the world.
"We are building Victors, not victims. That is the Promise."

Seffner to the World

Based in the vibrant Tampa Area, Florida, our mission begins here at home. Over the next five years, we are dedicated to creating a meaningful impact across the United States by funding local youth shelters and school JROTC programs.

But our vision is global. Once our foundation is firmly established in the U.S., we intend to extend our resources and support to communities in Haiti and Africa. With Pierre-Richard at the helm and his two sons by his side, the Victor Global Initiative is more than a foundation—it is a lasting family legacy of hope.
