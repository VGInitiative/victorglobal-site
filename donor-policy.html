Transparency & Trust

Donor Privacy & Gift Acceptance

Effective Date: March 2026

1. Our Commitment to Donor Privacy

The Victor Global Initiative (VGI) is committed to respecting the privacy of our financial partners. We recognize that your investment in our youth requires absolute trust.

  • No Trade or Sell Policy: We will not sell, share, or trade our donors' names or personal information with any other entity, nor send mailings to our donors on behalf of other organizations.
  • Data Collection: We collect donor information (name, email, address, and donation history) solely to process payments, distribute tax receipts, and keep you informed about our mission's impact.
  • Payment Security: Online donations are processed using enterprise-grade encryption via Stripe and the PayPal Giving Fund. VGI does not store sensitive credit card data on our internal servers.

2. Gift Acceptance

VGI is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (EIN: 41-3649085). All donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent permitted by law.

  • Accepted Assets: We gladly accept cash, credit/debit transactions, checks, and secure online transfers.
  • In-Kind Donations: Non-cash gifts (such as equipment for JROTC programs, professional attire for career prep, or shelter supplies) are accepted at the discretion of the Executive Board.
  • Restricted Gifts: Donors may request their funds be restricted to specific initiatives (e.g., Shelter Outreach). VGI will honor these restrictions or notify the donor if the restriction cannot be feasibly met before accepting the gift.

3. Financial Accountability & Oversight

Integrity is the foundation of our airframe. We operate under strict fiscal governance to ensure every dollar directly empowers at-risk youth and foster children.

  • Board Oversight: All financial operations, budgets, and donor allocations are actively overseen by the VGI Treasurer and the Board of Directors.
  • Transparency: Donors have the right to request and review our most recent financial statements and IRS Form 990 filings.

4. Donor Communications

We believe in keeping our partners informed without overwhelming their inboxes.

  • Donors will receive an official tax-compliant acknowledgment letter for their records.
  • Periodic updates regarding youth impact, milestones, and strategic initiatives will be shared.
  • Donors may opt out of non-essential communications at any time by contacting info@victorglobal.org.

5. Refund Policy

Due to the nature of charitable giving, donations to VGI are generally non-refundable. However, we will honor refund requests made within 30 days of the transaction if an error was made (e.g., a duplicate processing error or a typo in the donation amount).
