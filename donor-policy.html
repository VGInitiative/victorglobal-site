Transparency & Stewardship

Donor Privacy & Gift Acceptance

Effective Date: March 2026

At Victor Global Initiative (VGI), we recognize that your investment in our youth is a partnership built on absolute trust. We handle every contribution with the same professional integrity and "Aviation-Grade" discipline we apply to our mentorship programs.

1. Our Commitment to Donor Privacy

We treat our donors' information as a sacred oath. VGI maintains a strict policy regarding the data of our financial partners:

  • No Trade or Sell Policy: We will not sell, share, or trade our donors' names or personal information with any other entity, nor send mailings to our donors on behalf of other organizations.
  • Information Stewardship: We collect donor data solely to process payments, provide tax-compliant receipts, and share progress reports on the **Victor Mentorship Model**.
  • Secure Processing: Online donations are encrypted via Stripe. VGI does not store sensitive financial data on internal servers.

2. Gift Acceptance Standards

VGI is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (EIN: 41-3649085). Donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent permitted by law.

  • General Assets: We accept cash, secure online transfers, and checks directed to our Florida headquarters.
  • In-Kind Contributions: Gifts of professional attire, JROTC equipment, or vocational tools are accepted at the discretion of the Board of Directors to ensure they align with our **Dignity First** pillar.
  • Restricted Gifts: Donors may request funds be allocated to specific Phase 1 initiatives, such as **Shelter Outreach** or **JROTC Sponsorship**.

3. Financial Accountability & Oversight

Integrity is the foundation of our mission. To ensure that every dollar creates capability rather than dependency:

  • Board Governance: All budgets and donor allocations are overseen by the VGI Treasurer and the Board of Directors.
  • Transparency Rights: Our partners have the right to request and review our financial statements and annual IRS filings.

4. Refund Policy

Charitable contributions are generally non-refundable. However, VGI will honor refund requests made within 30 days of a transaction if a clear processing error has occurred (e.g., duplicate billing).
