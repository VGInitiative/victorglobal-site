Mandatory Requirements

  • JROTC Enrollment: Must be currently active in JROTC.
  • Community Service: Must provide proof of verifiable service hours.
  • GPA: Minimum 3.0 cumulative GPA.
  • Recommendations: Two (2) letters; one must be from an ROTC Instructor.
  • Essay: 500-750 words on "Transforming Trials into Triumph."

The "Victor" Evaluation Rubric

Essay (Leadership & Adversity): 40 pts

Leadership Roles: 25 pts

Community Service Impact: 20 pts

Academic Discipline (GPA): 10 pts

Letters of Recommendation: 5 pts

Total: 100 Points
