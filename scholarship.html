Eligibility

Full Requirements List

  • ✔ Be an active JROTC student
  • ✔ Submit a Photo in JROTC Uniform
  • ✔ Maintain a minimum 2.8 GPA
  • ✔ Demonstrate verifiable community service
  • ✔ Submit two recommendation letters (one from ROTC Instructor)
  • ✔ Complete a 500–750 word essay
  • ✔ Provide official school verification
  • ✔ Submit a Video Personal Impact Statement
Note: All scholarship funds are disbursed directly to the student's school of attendance or acceptance letter registrar.
Selection Criteria

The Victor Evaluation Rubric

Our selection committee scores candidates based on a professional 100-point discipline scale:

Essay & Video Statement 20 pts
Leadership & JROTC Service 30 pts
Community Service Impact 25 pts
Academic Discipline (GPA) 20 pts
Recommendations 5 pts
Total Score 100 Points
